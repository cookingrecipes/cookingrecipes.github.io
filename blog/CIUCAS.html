# Ciucas Mountains, Romania

Ciucas Mountains are one of the most impressive mountains in Romania, due to their unique rock formations. They are located between Brasov and Prahova counties and their maximum altitude is 1954m. There are many hiking trails reaching the top. One of the most beautiful starts in Pasul Bratocea. There is an easy car access up to that spot.

The structure is conglomerate, a classic sedimentary rock, composed of rounded to subangular gravel-size rocks.
